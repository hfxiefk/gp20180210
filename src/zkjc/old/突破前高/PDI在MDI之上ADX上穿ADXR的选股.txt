DIM中
PDI金叉MDI
或
PDI在MDI之上 ADX上穿ADXR的选股

回复在下面


















N:=14;
MM:=6;

MTR:=EXPMEMA(MAX(MAX(HIGH-LOW,ABS(HIGH-REF(CLOSE,1))),ABS(REF(CLOSE,1)-LOW)),N);
HD :=HIGH-REF(HIGH,1);
LD :=REF(LOW,1)-LOW;
DMP:=EXPMEMA(IF(HD>0&&HD>LD,HD,0),N);
DMM:=EXPMEMA(IF(LD>0&&LD>HD,LD,0),N);

PDI:=DMP*100/MTR;
MDI:=DMM*100/MTR;

ADX:=EXPMEMA(ABS(MDI-PDI)/(MDI+PDI)*100,MM);
ADXR:=EXPMEMA(ADX,MM);

TA:=CROSS(PDI,MDI);
TB:=PDI>MDI AND CROSS(ADX,ADXR);
XG:TA OR TB;
